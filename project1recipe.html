Simple Banana Bread Recipe

This simple banana bread recipe is healthy, easy to make and uses ingedients that you probably already have in your pantry!

Ingregients

  • 4 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs (beaten)
  • 1 tsp. vanilla
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups flour (1 cup whole wheat)
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Grease inside of a loaf pan with shortening
  3. In a large bowl, mash bananas
  4. In a separate bowl, beat eggs and then add to mashed bananas
  5. Combine sugar and oil to the banana mixture
  6. Stir together dry ingredients and add to banana mixture. Mix well
  7. Pour into prepared bread pan
  8. Bake 50-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean
